About Us

Dar ul Arqam

A non-profit dedicated to improving the lives of orphans in Pakistan. Our primary goal is to raise funds for the construction of an orphanage, providing a safe and nurturing environment for those in need. Through the generous support of donors, we strive to create a space where orphaned children can receive proper care, education, and support to build a brighter future. Your donations play a crucial role in making this vision a reality.

  • Transforming Umerabad: Abdul Qayum Khan and Dar ul Arqam Welfare organization join hands for lasting impact

    By Staff Reporter 11 December 2024

At a ceremony held on Monday, December 9, 2024, Mr. Khan officially donated a prime piece of land to DUA. This land will be used to construct essential welfare facilities to uplift the underprivileged.

    The event, attended by community leaders, Darul Arqam members, and local residents, began with a site visit to the donated property. The formal proceedings were held at the Said Akbar Khan Hujra, a historic and symbolic venue.

    "A New Dawn for Umerabad"

    In his address, Mr. Abdul Latif, Chairman of Darul Arqam Welfare Organization, expressed deep gratitude to Mr. Khan. He said:

    "This land has been part of my family for generations, and I feel privileged to dedicate it to a cause that will benefit the community for decades to come, Darul Arqam‚Äôs unwavering commitment to the welfare of orphans and the underprivileged inspired me to make this contribution.     The donation includes a clause ensuring that neither Mr. Khan nor his legal heirs will make any future claims on the land or the structures built upon it. He then added:
